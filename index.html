About

Khalanga Carpetry and General work is a multi-disciplinary Black-Owned Campany focussing on Carpentry and General Work. Our main goal is to ensure our clients are happy with our work. We don't consider a job complete until our client is completely satisfied with the quality of our service. As a small team servicing clients in various places we understand the value of customer satisfaction. Our focus is on keeping overheads to a minimum so we are able to offer quality work for reasonable prices.
